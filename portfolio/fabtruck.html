FAB TRUCK

Mobile fab lab platform with standart digital fabrication equipment.

Being fab labs part of the 4 industrial revolution, what if a fabrication laboratory could have mobility to reach any event of neighborhood. Based on that premise the Fabtruck was developed in put in function to assist to the FabLab Seoul event around South Korea.


Equipment of the mobile Fab Truck

  • Small table size CNC
  • Cameo vinyl cutter.
  • 3d normal size FDM 3d printers
  • medium size C02 lasercutter.
  • Fabrication materials stock
  • Portable power generator
  • Promotional materials
  • 3 computer laptops

Exterior design



Interior design



The complete interior design furniture was produced with plywood in a screwless design using only digital fabrication wood joinery.
