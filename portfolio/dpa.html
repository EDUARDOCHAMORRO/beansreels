Beans Reels

DIGITAL prototyping for Architecture

course in the Master in Advanced Ecological Buildings at IAAC.

The aforementioned structures are composed of several modules generated with digital fabrication equipment in groups of 4 students, creating multiscalar interconnections with their environment in one unique result.


Final project of DPA subject in the Master of Advanced Ecological Buildings at IaaC. 2018-2019 Student designed and built several structures taking as reference mushrooms organisms found in the surroundings of Valldaura Green FabLab. Each structure resembles or projects the morphology of the mushrooms selected by each team.
