The concept of the Fab Guetto Blaster is to lets you make all the parts so you can replace, customize,pimp... based on your needs. The lightweight and portable package make it stronger. Using a very efficient class D amplifier allows this magic box to last at least 60 hours of NON STOP

Let's Start!
Build yours

Get all the components. Here is the B.O.M for multiple configurations

Choose
the version

Learn which version you want to make

Fixing
the battery

Put a little bit of double side tape/velcro o hot glue behind the battery case or lipo

Inside
panel

Introduce the middle panel in the frame and fits it in place

Fix
Inside panel

Use four screws 3 mm without washer to fix the panel.

Battery
position

Unstick the double side tape and check that it fits inside from the back.

Wire
placing

Introduce the wires from the battery on the side holes between the inner plate and the frame

Back
panels

Fit the battery and test the position of the back panels to allow you replacing the battery.

Fixing
back panels

Use four screws of 3 mm and washers to fix the back panels.

Front
Panel

Fix the speaker with two screws,washer and nuts with the fixing ring or just glue the speaker on the profile.

Switch
placing

After turn face up the front panel introduce the switch by pressure,it should fit smoothly.

Jack
Only wired version

Introduce the stereo jack wire from the outside back panel to the inside,leave a maximun of 30cm outside.

Jack
Fix it

Make a knot on the wire to unallow it to stress the electronic.

Front panel
Wiring

Solder the connections between the amplifier to switch and speaker.If you have the potentiometer version screw it trought the front panel.

Battery
Connection

Solder the connection from the battery to the switch and amplifier "be carefull to don't make shorts".

Stereo
Soldering

Solder the wires coming from the bluetooth or the stereo jack.Ground is in the middle position.Check all the solders looking for shorts.

Fixing
the electronic

Fix the rest of the components to the frame of the speaker with hot glue

Final
Assembly

Close the front panel with four screws of 3 mm and washers.

Set
The music loud!

Put the batteries and let's start dancing!.
