Manifesto

Beans Reels is a one-man studio operated by Eduardo Chamorro

I am interested in how technology and new trends can transform architecture and his processes to improve peoples lives.I seek to push myself, past my potential and develop new architectural methods (and/or human centered design processes) thatcould be applied in to the real world meanwhile sharing theses into the community.

MY DESIGN PROCESS

Improving new ways of communication, materials, develop unique procedures of creation in the process to achieve a complete innovative architecture that solves and adapts to the society needs in a multi-scalar environment must be the priority in an architect nowadays.

Therefore I have always sought to obtain and complement my education among multidisciplinary fields and environments
